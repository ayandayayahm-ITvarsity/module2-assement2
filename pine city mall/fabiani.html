Fabiani



Where is Fabiani brand from? “What is great about the Fabiani business is that it is born in South Africa with an Italian heritage. Right now, we are probably the most powerful and aspirational premium brand in the country,” says Fabian.



Fabiani is South Africa's premier destination for sophisticated luxury menswear. Established in 1978, the brand has a passionate devotion to Italian-inspired design and tailoring as well as unique detailing, superior fabrication and bold colours.
